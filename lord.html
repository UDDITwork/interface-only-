Introduction to Robotics

Robotics is an interdisciplinary field that integrates computer science, engineering, and AI to design, construct, and operate intelligent machines known as robots. These robots can perform tasks autonomously or under human guidance, making them essential in modern industries and research.

Essential Algorithms in Robotics

Algorithms are the backbone of robotics, dictating how robots process information, make decisions, and interact with the environment. From pathfinding to machine learning, each algorithm plays a crucial role in making robots intelligent and adaptable.
